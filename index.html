    We will start with the history of one metal-man, the history of Tobias Sammet, he is the singer of two groups, Avantasia; until 2000 year and Edguy; started in 1992 year (the year that was publicated "fear of the the dark").     He borned in the year 1977, in the city of Fulda (Germany) he started to hear Heavy Metal with "the keeper of the seven keys" the album of "Helloween", at the age of ten, Tobbias met Jens Ludwing, the future guitar player of Edguy, Jens liked Heavy Metal too, they were very good friends.     When he had the twelve years old, he started to compose their own songs a little bit based in Helloween's songs. With fourteen years he wanted to make a group with Jens, his name was called with his name because his teacher of the high school had the nickname of "Edguy". The first album that composed was "The savatage poetry" but, the first album that publicated was "The kingdom of madness" because they haven't money to public it. Then, he created Avantasia with "the Metal opera" and before it, he abandoned a little bit Edguy.
